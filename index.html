Our Services

Group Workout

Weekly group sessions for mums to foster the workout spirit and share different experiences on losing post-partum weight. These sessions will help both new mums and aged with highly needed motivation and healthy results.

Nutritionists

With the right workout plans, there are also well-planned out diets for all mums to get back in shape fast and healthily. Our nutritionists are well-trained and will ensure your nutrition is healthy for your body as a mother and also for your nursing babies.

Health Coaching

From the best of fitness instructors and health advisors, mums are sure to get the best advice, instructions and routines to help them get back in shape. Our coaches are expertly trained and always ready to help at your beck and call.

Online Classes

Online classes to ensure continued routine for mums who can't make it to the gym physically and also network with the other mums from the different categories. This service is available to all from all regions.
