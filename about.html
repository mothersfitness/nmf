ABOUT US

New Mothers Fitness

Is an initiative of the Bisonel Gyms and Fitness Organization created in the year 2020. The program is targeted at new mums to help them get back in shape through detailed and expert-approved workout and diet plans. This initiative also puts mums who are done with child bearing but want to stay in shape through their daily activities. New Mothers Fitness engages the services of fitness trainers, health advisors and dieticians all over the country to ensure the best for all our fantastic mums. Our headquarters is at Ogba, Lagos, and our service lines are open 24/7 for your requests, enquiries, and complaints. Stay fit and healthy!
